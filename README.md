# SMSV Korban Ministry – Bread Calculator

A mobile-friendly web calculator for preparing Korban (Orban) bread for liturgical services at SMSV church.

## What It Does

- Select your liturgy day (Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday, Sunday) to auto-fill the recommended dry flour amount
- Choose between **Scalding Dough** mode (Batch 1 preparation) or **Full Bread Recipe** mode (Batch 2 mix)
- Automatically splits large batches into mixer loads based on a 15 kg dry flour capacity
- Displays per-load ingredient amounts including dry flour, water, yeast, and scald dough weight
- Supports light and dark mode based on device settings

## Notes

- All recipes use Costco flour
- Calculations based on 40% scalding ratio and 65% water hydration
- Yeast quantities are based on summer measurements
