Step-by-Step Instructions
Preparation
  • P
    Turn on Proofer — set humidity to level 9 and temperature to 110°F.
  • P
    Prepare tray(s), table, tools, and all necessary equipment.
Scalding Dough (Batch 1)
  • 1
    Determine batch size — see the liturgy day selector above for reference amounts.
  • 2
    Prepare the Scald using the 40% scald ratio amounts shown in the calculator.
  • 3
    Use boiling water for the scald. Mix for at least 15–20 minutes.
  • 4
    Cool the scald — it must be fully and thoroughly cooled before continuing. Do not rush this step; incomplete cooling will ruin the batch.
    Tip: point a fan at the mixer bowl and periodically run the mixer to move the dough and ensure even cooling.
Bread Dough (Batch 2)
  • 1
    Add the remaining flour, cold water, and yeast (per the calculator amounts) to the fully cooled scald.
  • 2
    Mix the combined dough for 10–12 minutes.
  • 3
    Check dough softness — it should be smooth and soft. If needed, add a small amount of water and re-mix for 3 more minutes.
Shaping & Proofing
  • 4
    Remove dough and portion to the desired loaf size.
  • 5
    Shape, stamp, and place each loaf in the baking tray(s).
  • 6
    Once all loaves are in trays, place in the proofer:
     • 55–60 min for loaves larger than 1000 g
     • 35–45 min for smaller loaves
  • 7
    30 minutes after placing in the proofer, turn on oven(s) and preheat to 365°F.
  • 8
    Check proofing progress and add time if needed.
Baking
  • 9
    When ready, remove from proofer and poke 5 holes in each loaf.
  • 10
    Place loaves in the oven and set timer for 22 minutes.
  • 11
    After 5 minutes in the oven, remove loaves and re-poke the holes, then return to finish baking.
  • 12
    Bake until the full 22 minutes is complete. Target color: lightly golden, fully baked, not crispy.
Finishing
  • 13
    Set loaves to cool. Handle Hamal Orban with extra care and place at the front.
  • 14
    Cover loaves with a clean cloth to retain moisture.
  • 15
    Clean the Orban room — equipment, tools, and floor. Return stamps, rollers, and all items to designated storage.
  • 16
    Ensure all equipment (ovens, proofers) are off, and lock the Orban room.
  • 17
    Report flour and yeast inventory to the Orban leaders group.
  • 18
    🙏 God bless your service and reward everyone with heavenly blessings.
⚠️ Important: Only use Costco flour for this recipe. Results may vary with other flour brands.
